The unlock
One init. Every agent reads
the same project state.

Claude works your frontend. Gemini audits your architecture. Grok stress-tests your assumptions. All three dispatched from one job queue, writing to one shared ledger — no repeated setup, no lost state.

Token economics
Sticking to one model concentrates your token spend, hits rate limits faster, and locks out model diversity. Spread work across agents — spread cost across providers. The workgroup model is cheaper and richer.
